introduction

Sri Venkateshwaraa College of Engineering & Technology (SVCET) is a vibrant Institute of higher education established in the year 2014 with the objective of producing globally competitive and ethical Engineers with Industrial exposure. SVCET is affiliated to Pondicherry University and approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi. The College campus nestles amidst calm and clean campus sprawling 10.5 acres situated at Ariyur which is about 12 km from Pondicherry. The College operates a fleet of buses connecting all parts of the city for the convenience of the Students and Staff. Besides this, the College is well connected to different parts of the city by public transport buses.Sri Venkateshwaraa College of Engineering and Technology hosted a grand Graduation Day ceremony on the 16th of July 2023. The event, held at the Sai Auditorium in Sri Venkateshwaraa College Integrated Campus, marked a significant milestone in the lives of the graduating B.Tech students. Batch : 3rd 2016-20 The highlight of the ceremony was the awarding of degrees to the graduands. With pride and a sense of accomplishment, each graduate walked across the stage, receiving their well-earned degree, marking the culmination of their educational journey. The graduates, filled with a sense of responsibility, took the Graduation Day Oath, pledging to uphold the values of their respective professions, serve society, and make a positive impact on the world.
